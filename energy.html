FAQ

Solar questions.

Anything else — the monitoring desk answers around the clock.

Do you install, or only engineer?+

We are the engineer of record and construction overseer. Installation is executed by vetted EPC partners working to our drawings, with EXA field engineers verifying every stage — pile refusal, torque, and string commissioning included.

Can my roof take a solar array?+

That's the first thing we check. A structural residual-capacity assessment of the host building precedes any rooftop layout — if the roof can't carry the system through its design life, we say so before you spend anything on modules.

How accurate are your yield forecasts?+

Fleet-wide, first-year production has landed within ±2.1% of our P50 forecasts. Every model is certified to IEC 61724-1 and backed by on-site pyranometer data where available.

What happens after commissioning?+

The array feeds our 24/7 monitoring desk. Underperformance against the yield model triggers a ticket within the hour, and every site receives an annual drone thermographic inspection.
