How We Deliver

A four-stage sequence, every time.

No shortcuts between site data and signed-off structure. Each stage closes with a written sign-off before the next begins.

STAGE 01

Survey & Site Data

Geotechnical surveys, LiDAR scans, and load-history data collected on site.

STAGE 02

Structural Modeling

Finite-element analysis against seismic, wind, and live-load scenarios.

STAGE 03

Validation & Testing

Independent peer review and physical load-testing of critical assemblies.

STAGE 04

Construction Oversight

On-site inspection through to final commissioning and as-built sign-off.

FAQ

Common questions.

Don't see your question here — reach the operations desk directly.

What project sizes do you take on?+

From single-structure feasibility studies to multi-hundred-megawatt energy programs. Scope is set in the first consultation, and every engagement gets the same peer-review process regardless of size.

Which standards do your calculations follow?+

ASCE 7, Eurocode, IEC 61400, and DNV standards depending on jurisdiction and asset type — always the local governing code plus our own internal margin above it.

Do you provide construction-phase oversight?+

Yes — Stage 04 of our process places a field engineer on site through commissioning, with weekly as-built reconciliation against the approved model.

How quickly can you start a feasibility review?+

Typically within two weeks of a signed engagement letter, sooner for existing clients with data already on file with our operations desk.
